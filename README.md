📊 NorthStar Living UK – Retail Performance Dashboard
🔍 Project Overview

This project analyses the retail performance of NorthStar Living UK, a multi-channel business operating across online and in-store platforms.

The goal was to identify key drivers of revenue, profitability, customer behaviour, and operational performance, and provide actionable insights to support better business decisions.

🎯 Business Objectives
Understand revenue and profit performance across regions
Analyse product category contribution to sales and profitability
Evaluate the impact of discounting on margins
Identify high-value customer segments
Investigate return patterns and operational inefficiencies
Assess the relationship between delivery performance and customer satisfaction
📌 Key KPIs
Total Revenue: £733K
Profit Margin: 31%
Return Rate: 3.66%
Customer Satisfaction Score: 4.17
📊 Dashboard Features
Revenue & Profit analysis by region and time
Product category and subcategory performance
Customer segmentation insights
Discount vs Profitability analysis
Returns analysis by category and channel
Delivery time vs Customer Satisfaction correlation
💡 Key Insights
High revenue does not always lead to high profit due to discounting
Certain regions generate strong sales but lower margins
Furniture and kitchen categories drive revenue, but not always profit
The consumer segment is the most valuable customer group
Returns are concentrated in specific categories (e.g., furniture, personal care)
Longer delivery times negatively impact customer satisfaction
✅ Recommendations
Optimise discount strategies to protect profit margins
Investigate high-return categories to reduce losses
Improve delivery performance to enhance customer experience
Focus marketing on high-value customer segments
Monitor profitability alongside revenue for better decision-making
