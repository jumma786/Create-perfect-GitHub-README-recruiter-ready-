# 📊 NorthStar Living UK – Retail Performance Dashboard

## 🔍 Project Overview  
This project analyses the retail performance of NorthStar Living UK, a multi-channel business operating across online and in-store platforms.  

The objective is to identify key drivers of revenue, profitability, customer behaviour, and operational performance, and provide actionable insights to support better business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives  
- Analyse revenue and profit across regions  
- Identify top-performing product categories  
- Evaluate the impact of discounting on profitability  
- Understand customer segment value  
- Investigate return patterns  
- Assess delivery performance and customer satisfaction  

---

## 📌 Key KPIs  
- **Total Revenue:** £733K  
- **Profit Margin:** 31%  
- **Return Rate:** 3.66%  
- **Customer Satisfaction:** 4.17  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features  
- Revenue & Profit analysis by region and time  
- Product category and subcategory performance  
- Customer segmentation insights  
- Discount vs Profitability analysis  
- Returns analysis by category  
- Delivery time vs Customer Satisfaction  

---

## 💡 Key Insights  
- High revenue does not always mean high profit due to discounting  
- Some regions generate strong sales but lower margins  
- Furniture and kitchen categories drive revenue, but not always profit  
- The consumer segment is the most valuable customer group  
- Returns are higher in specific categories like furniture  
- Longer delivery times reduce customer satisfaction  

---

## ✅ Recommendations  
- Optimise discount strategies to improve margins  
- Reduce returns in high-return categories  
- Improve delivery efficiency  
- Focus on high-value customer segments  
- Track profitability alongside revenue
